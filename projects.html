CLUB PROJECTS :

Check out our Club's Projects below.

SANDSTONE POINT COMMUNITY CAROLS  -  WE LOOK FORWARD TO BRINGING THIS WONDERFUL COMMUNITY EVENT BACK FOR THE COMMUNITY ON 11TH DECEMBER 2021

Our Club in conjunction with the Sandstone Point Community Association proudly hosts the annual Sandstone Point Community Carols.   The event provides free entry and free rides for the children including climbing walls, animal farm, jumping castles, merrygoround, face painting.   Food stalls are available throughout the day and evening.   The day culminates in a Community Christmas singalong at the end of the evening with a local choir and solo artists   

YOUTH OF THE YEAR : A LIONS public speaking program for year 11 and 12 students. Students  speak for 5 minutes on a subject of their choice and then 2 minutes on impromptu current affairs questions.  The competition progresses through Club, Zone, District, State and National Level.  Members of the Public are welcome to come along and listen to the students speak.  Our Club is proudly  associated with St Columban's College, Cabooluture.  

2021 WINNER LIAM TORY with Judging Panel (right) and 2021 Competitors (left)

 

CHILDREN OF COURAGE AWARDS :  A program to honour children with special needs such as children who have shown courage in the face of adversity; have been instrumental in the preservation of life or property; have rendered assistance at an accident or whose concern for others is worthy of recognition; have achieved a high degree of sporting prowess or may have put in a massive effort to struggle through school and meet day to day needs.   IF YOU KNOW OF A CHILD WORTHY OF RECOGNITION PLEASE CONTACT OUR CLUB.    Due to Covid this event was not held in 2021 but we are hopeful of holding this event again in 2021.

                                                                         

AUSTRALIA DAY CLEAN UP : Once a year members gather together to clean up the local area.

 

EYE GLASS RECYCLING : A Lions project where volunteers assist in the cleaning and grading of glasses to be send overseas.  Three of our members regularly attend each week. 

TRADITIONAL CHRISTMAS CAKE AND PUDDING SALES :  An annual project where Lions Christmas Cakes and Puddings are sold within the community.  

ICED CHRISTMAS CAKES AND PUDDINGS ; A group of members get together each to decorate and bake up 'mini' and 'muffin' Christmas Cakes.  These cakes have become very popular as a lovely gift for family, friends, neighbours, teachers etc.

DEFIBRILLATER DONATED TO LOCAL SHOPPING CENTRE :  Our Club recently, at the request of the local medical centre, and with thanks to a grant for 50% of purchase price to Australian Lions Foundation, recently installed at defibrillator in IGA, Sandstone Point.  The attached photo shows Secretary Andy Fitzgerald, President Len Dawber and Councillor and Member Brooke Savige presenting the defibrillator to Lorraine from medical centre, Jason (store manager) and Adrian from IGA.
