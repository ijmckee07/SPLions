ABOUT OUR CLUB

We like to think of ourselves as the 'fun' Club as our members are involved in many different social and fun activities as well as our Club and Lions projects.

Our Club was formed in April 2015 as a Branch Club of Morayfield & District Lions Club after Lion Wendy Rollason saw a need for a new Club in our region.     It took just under one year for our Club to be chartered in its own right on 14th March 2016.      A celebration dinner was held on 16th April 2016 where Wendy Rollason accepted the honour of being the Charter President of our Club.

We are very proud of our Club and what we have achieved in our now 5 years.  We have grown to nearlly 40 members  who come together regularly for fun and fellowship at the Sandstone Point Community Hall, 202 Bestmann Road East, Sandstone Point.

Realizing the need to support other growing Clubs, our Club agreed in 2017 to sponsor the formation of a new Branch Club; the Lions Club of Caloundra West who chartered in their own right in 2018.

Lions Clubs are part of community life, in the cities and in the country. Clubs are easily identified by their distinctive Logo, the trademark of our International Association, and the 'We Serve'; motto. 

Lions Australia is part of an international association, filled with people who are joined by the common desire to make their communities better, by using their creativity, enthusiasm and energy.

Membership is open to all people of the community in good standing.
